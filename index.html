Our Mission

Bits-on-Wheels acts as an independent repository dedicated to exploring smart vehicle architecture, telemetry frameworks, and software-defined physical ecosystems. Our core focus lies in dissecting how physical mobility syncs with processing logic, establishing stable knowledge systems free from corporate silos.

Decentralized Data Streams

Analyzing high-throughput data pipelines and secure telemetry distribution structures across complex network environments.

Embedded Security Matrix

Investigating mitigation architectures against vectors targeting interconnected IoT frameworks and local field busses.
